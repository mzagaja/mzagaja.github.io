As Senior Backend Engineer at TED, I work on the backend systems that power TED.com and its content delivery infrastructure.

vote.ctnewsjunkie.com

A civic tech project built in collaboration with CTNewsJunkie that makes Connecticut voting records accessible and searchable. The goal is to increase civic engagement by making it easier for residents to track how their elected officials vote. View project.

bills.ctnewsjunkie.com

A companion civic tech project with CTNewsJunkie that tracks Connecticut legislative bills, making it easier for residents to follow the progress of legislation that affects them. View project.
